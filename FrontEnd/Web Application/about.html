About Our Project

AI-based crop disease detection leverages advanced technologies such as machine learning, computer vision, and deep learning to identify and diagnose plant diseases accurately and efficiently.

This not only helps farmers take timely action to prevent further spread but also reduces the dependency on harmful chemical pesticides by enabling targeted treatments.
